About Perspective Tester

Our Mission: Engineering Equity

Perspective Tester is devoted to engineering equity for the digital world. Accessibility is a fundamental right, not an afterthought. We help organizations serving approximately 2 billion people globally who require support when using digital platforms. Our team of IAAP-certified accessibility professionals and native assistive technology users conducts practical, real-world testing.

Leadership Team

Jason McKee — Managing Director, CEO & Founder. Visionary leader driving the mission of digital accessibility for all.

Justin Salas — Accounts Manager & Accessibility Champion. A native assistive technology user who brings lived experience to every project.

Khushwant Parihar — Web Accessibility Specialist. Expert in WCAG compliance, remediation, and accessible development.

Our Approach: 4-Phase Methodology

Our proven methodology ensures comprehensive accessibility:

Step 1: Audit — Full-site accessibility audit with assistive technology testing

Step 2: Report — Detailed WCAG 2.2 compliance report with prioritized issues

Step 3: Remediate — Expert-led remediation with code-level fixes and guidance

Step 4: Protect — Ongoing monitoring and legal protection guarantee

Government Distribution via Carahsoft

Perspective Tester partners with Carahsoft Technology Corp., the trusted IT solutions provider, to deliver accessibility solutions to the public sector. This partnership provides government agencies with streamlined procurement through established contract vehicles, making it easy to access enterprise accessibility testing and compliance services.
