P15R — Accessibility Ops Platform

The operations platform for accessibility teams. Track, assign, and resolve accessibility issues across websites and applications with real-time dashboards, vendor collaboration, and deep integrations into your development workflow.

P15R

Accessibility Ops Platform

Centralize your accessibility operations. From audit intake to issue resolution, P15R gives your team the visibility and tools to deliver accessible digital experiences at scale.

Start Free Trial

Platform Capabilities

Audit Intake

Upload audit reports from any source. P15R automatically parses issues, maps them to WCAG criteria, and creates actionable tickets for your team.

Real-Time Dashboards

Track compliance scores, issue velocity, resolution times, and team performance across all your digital properties in a single view.

Vendor Collaboration

Invite external vendors and accessibility consultants to collaborate directly in the platform with scoped access and audit trails.

Jira Integration

Bidirectional sync with Jira. Create Jira tickets from P15R issues, and status changes sync automatically in both directions.

Slack & Teams

Get real-time notifications in Slack or Microsoft Teams. Assign issues, update statuses, and collaborate without leaving your chat tool.

Compliance Reporting

Generate VPAT/ACR reports, executive summaries, and compliance trend reports on demand. Export in PDF, CSV, or share via link.
