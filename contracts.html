Accessibility Compliance Standards

Understanding Digital Accessibility Requirements

Perspective Tester helps organizations navigate the complex landscape of digital accessibility regulations. Our team of IAAP-certified professionals ensures your digital properties meet all applicable standards and protect your organization from legal risk.

WCAG 2.2 AA

Web Content Accessibility Guidelines W3C International Standard The global standard for web accessibility covering perceivable, operable, understandable, and robust principles.
PT Approach Full manual + automated testing against all 50+ success criteria. Detailed remediation guidance with code-level fixes.

Section 508

Rehabilitation Act, Section 508 Federal Requirement Requires federal agencies to make electronic and information technology accessible to people with disabilities.
PT Approach VPAT/ACR documentation, assistive technology testing, and ongoing monitoring for federal compliance.

ADA Title II

Americans with Disabilities Act State & Local Government Requires state and local governments to make their digital services accessible. April 2026 compliance deadline for many entities.
PT Approach End-to-end compliance program with A3S managed service specifically designed for government websites.

Section 504

Rehabilitation Act, Section 504 Federal Funding Recipients Applies to any organization receiving federal financial assistance, including schools, hospitals, and nonprofits.
PT Approach Comprehensive audits and remediation for education and healthcare organizations receiving federal funds.
