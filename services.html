Our Services

Perspective Tester offers a comprehensive suite of accessibility services designed to help organizations achieve and maintain digital accessibility compliance. From initial audits to ongoing monitoring, our IAAP-certified specialists deliver expert guidance at every stage.

Accessibility Auditing & Testing

Our comprehensive accessibility audits combine automated scanning with expert manual testing to identify every barrier in your digital experience. We test against WCAG 2.2 AA, Section 508, and ADA Title II requirements using both assistive technology and expert evaluation.

Full WCAG 2.2 AA audit
Screen reader testing (NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver)
Keyboard navigation review
Color contrast analysis
Mobile accessibility testing
Prioritized remediation roadmap
Request an Audit

Remediation Services

Our expert development team fixes accessibility issues directly in your codebase, CMS, or document library. We handle the technical remediation so your team can focus on core business objectives.

HTML/CSS/ARIA code fixes
CMS content remediation
Form & interactive element fixes
Multimedia alternatives

PDF & Document Remediation

We remediate PDFs, Word documents, and spreadsheets to meet WCAG 2.2 AA and PDF/UA standards. Every downloadable document on your site becomes fully accessible with proper reading order, alt text, form fields, and heading structure.

PDF/UA compliance
Reading order optimization
Alt text for all images
Accessible form fields

VPAT & ACR Documentation
