About Perspective Tester

Engineering Equity for the Digital World

We believe every person deserves equal access to digital experiences. Perspective Tester exists to make that belief a reality for government agencies, educational institutions, healthcare organizations, and enterprises worldwide.

Our Story

Perspective Tester was founded with a clear mission: to make digital accessibility achievable for every organization, regardless of size or technical capacity. We recognized that many organizations — especially in the public sector — faced significant challenges in meeting WCAG, Section 508, and ADA compliance requirements. The technical complexity, shortage of qualified accessibility professionals, and evolving legal landscape created barriers that left millions of users unable to access critical digital services.

Today, Perspective Tester serves over 150 government clients, has audited 500+ websites, and has remediated more than 25,000 accessibility issues. Our team of IAAP-certified accessibility professionals combines deep technical expertise with a genuine passion for inclusive design.

Our Values

Accessibility First

Everything we build and every decision we make starts with the question: does this improve accessibility for end users? Compliance is the baseline — true inclusion is the goal.

Partnership

We work alongside our clients as long-term partners, not one-time vendors. Your accessibility goals become our goals, and your success is our success.

Excellence

Our IAAP-certified professionals bring deep technical expertise and genuine care to every engagement. We don't cut corners — every audit, remediation, and report meets the highest standards.

Innovation

We continuously evolve our products and services with AI-powered scanning, real-time monitoring, and developer-first integrations that make accessibility a natural part of every workflow.

Our Partnership with Carahsoft

Perspective Tester is proudly distributed through Carahsoft Technology Corp., the Trusted IT Solutions Provider. This partnership makes our accessibility solutions available through established government procurement vehicles including GSA Schedule, enabling streamlined purchasing for federal, state, and local government agencies.

Through Carahsoft, government organizations can access Perspective Tester's full suite of accessibility products and services with simplified contracting, competitive pricing, and dedicated public sector support.
