Let The Leaders in ADA Web Compliance Solve Your Accessibility Issues!

Make your websites and digital assets accessible with Perspective Tester's industry-leading approach to ADA Web compliance. Meet all federal and state requirements, including the newly-updated ADA Title II rules published in April 2024! Perspective Tester utilizes manual testing with assistive technologies - the ONLY method approved by both the Department of Justice and the World Wide Web Consortium (the creators of the WCAG standards).

We provide full remediation services - a complete turn-key solution for accessibility compliance. Achieve compliance for your websites, web systems, payment portals, mobile apps, custom software applications, documents, video content and more! Our services also include document remediation for PDFs, Word files, spreadsheets, and presentations, ensuring all your digital assets meet federal accessibility standards. Perspective Tester is your one-stop shop for all things related to digital accessibility.

How Perspective Tester Ensures Digital Accessibility Through Two Core Products:

  • A3S (Accessibility as a Service) is a managed accessibility service for local and county governments, featuring a 4-phase workflow with VPAT reports and continuous monitoring.
  • P15R Platform is an accessibility operations platform that helps teams track, assign, and resolve accessibility issues with real-time collaboration and developer integrations.

Together, these solutions help organizations build accessible digital experiences, achieve compliance, and protect against legal risk.
