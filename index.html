Comprehensive Accessibility Solutions for Every Organization

Perspective Tester's enterprise accessibility platform enables organizations to test, monitor, and manage accessibility across their digital products. Whether serving government agencies, educational institutions, or healthcare organizations, Perspective Tester helps achieve and maintain WCAG 2.2 AA compliance with a combination of AI-powered technology and expert human testing by IAAP-certified accessibility professionals.

Carahsoft, the trusted IT solutions provider, partners with Perspective Tester to deliver accessibility compliance solutions to the public sector. This collaboration ensures government agencies can meet Section 508, ADA Title II, and WCAG 2.2 requirements. Perspective Tester solutions are available through GSA Schedule and other government procurement vehicles.

How Perspective Tester Ensures Digital Accessibility Through Two Core Products:

  • A3S (Accessibility as a Service) is a managed accessibility service for local and county governments, featuring a 4-phase workflow with VPAT reports and continuous monitoring.
  • P15R Platform is an accessibility operations platform that helps teams track, assign, and resolve accessibility issues with real-time collaboration and developer integrations.

Together, these solutions help organizations build accessible digital experiences, achieve compliance, and protect against legal risk.
