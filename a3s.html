A3S - Accessibility as a Service

Our A3S program was born to facilitate two things: ensuring legal compliance and reducing barriers to digital accessibility services. We developed these pillars after learning about the needs of municipalities in Colorado facing compliance standards from House Bill HB21-1110.

This program gained traction with municipalities limited by budget constraints and manpower. We now proudly serve several clients with a high degree of satisfaction.
