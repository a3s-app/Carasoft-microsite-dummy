The A3S 4-Phase Workflow

Phase 1: Audit

Comprehensive WCAG 2.2 AA audit of your website, applications, and digital documents. Combines automated scanning with expert manual testing by IAAP-certified professionals. Covers all WCAG success criteria including keyboard navigation, screen reader compatibility, and color contrast.

Phase 2: Report

Detailed accessibility report with VPAT/ACR documentation, prioritized issue list, and remediation roadmap. Each issue includes severity level, affected WCAG criteria, screenshots, and step-by-step remediation instructions for your development team.

Phase 3: Remediate

Our expert team fixes identified accessibility issues directly in your codebase or CMS. We handle HTML/CSS/ARIA remediation, PDF document fixes, form accessibility, multimedia alternatives, and navigation improvements. Full verification testing after each remediation cycle.

Phase 4: Protect

Ongoing monitoring and continuous compliance assurance. Automated weekly scans detect regressions, quarterly expert reviews verify compliance, and legal documentation stays current. Includes an accessibility statement for your website and incident response support.
