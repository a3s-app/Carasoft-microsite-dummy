Perspective Tester Products

Two powerful products designed to solve accessibility compliance from different angles. Use A3S when you need a fully managed service, or P15R when you need an operations platform for your internal team.

A3S — Accessibility as a Service

A managed accessibility service for local and county governments. We pair expert accessibility teams with public-sector workflows to help you reach and maintain WCAG 2.2 AA compliance with legal protection documentation. Features a 4-phase workflow: Audit, Report, Remediate, Protect. Starting at $1,500/month for sites up to 25 pages.

LEARN MORE

P15R — Accessibility Ops Platform

An accessibility operations platform that helps teams track, assign, and resolve accessibility issues across websites and applications. Features audit intake, real-time dashboards, vendor collaboration, and bidirectional Jira, Slack, and Teams integrations. $99/month per seat with unlimited uploads and issues.
